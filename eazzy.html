■ Application Architecture & Core Offerings

Eazzy Food takes pride in offering a highly convenient and efficient food delivery ecosystem directly connecting users with local restaurants:

❖ Simple Ordering & Checkout

Engineered intuitive online ordering flows. Users can easily browse dynamic menus, customize their meal preferences, and execute secure checkouts in just a few taps.

❖ Real-Time Order Tracking

Integrated a live mapping and notification system. Customers receive step-by-step updates—from kitchen preparation to the driver navigating to their front door.

❖ Flexible Logistics

Accommodates unique scheduling requirements by providing both instant on-demand deliveries and scheduled drop-offs for events or busy work days.

❖ Dynamic Promotions Engine

Built infrastructure to support special offers, exclusive seasonal discounts, and automated loyalty rewards to enhance customer retention.
