■ Application Architecture & Core Offerings

Vento is built for people who want complete control over their finances, combining deep customization, detailed insights, and zero compromise on data privacy:

❖ Offline-First Encrypted Database

No mandatory cloud accounts or data harvesting. All expense tracking remains fully encrypted on the user's local device, with secure CSV/JSON export/import options.

❖ Advanced Budgeting & Subscriptions

Users can track recurring bills and renewals in one place. Engineered real-time alerts that warn users before category limits are breached.

❖ Deep Analytics & Insights

Visualized reports transform raw expense data into actionable financial clarity, connecting daily spending habits to broader financial goals.

❖ Frictionless Custom UI/UX

One-tap transaction logging. Implemented deep customization logic allowing custom tags, subcategories, and precision search filters across multiple accounts.
